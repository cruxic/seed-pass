Seed Pass
Welcome! To get started you need a "seed". The seed is a piece of random data that is unique to you and nobody else. All your passwords are calculated from this seed.

Because the seed is the source of all your passwords it needs to be guarded carefully. Only do the following steps on a computer you trust.

Your new seed has been encrypted with your PIN and is ready to use. You should print this and put it in a safe location.

Your Seed will be encrypted with a password. This protects you in case your USB device or printed Seed is lost or stolen.
  1. Use at least 8 characers.
  2. Use a password that you can remember. If you forget it, there is no way to recover your seed!
  3. Longer passwords tend to be stronger and easier to remember than shorter ones having capitalization, numbers and special characters.
  4. You will only have to type this password once at each computer which you use Seed-Pass.
  5. If you use a password hint, keep it vague.
